When will we apply the changes?
We will apply the changes to your direct debit from the next available due date, if this is less than 5 working days it may defer to the next due date. If your premium is currently overdue, we will provide you with written notice with the amount and date prior to deducting any payments.

    1. At any time, terminate this authority as to future payment by giving written notice of termination to both the Bank and the Initiator.
